About Us

Orther Mulelid-Tynes Technologies Inc. (OMTT) is a multinational development company with a focus on joint ventures with people who have great ideas. You provide the business idea and we provide the means to make the product.

Between us we have over 25 years of development experience and we can take on all kinds of development projects. We generally look for businesses or people who have a small or non-existent development and/or IT operations department. We then work closely with you to bring the idea all the way from inception to finished product. Along the way we build a long term relationship keeping system operations running after the product goes to market and further develop the product as it needs to evolve.

Projects

Safaridb

Safaridb is a project aiming to make a complete database of geological outcrops around the world. Gathering detailed 3d-models, measurements and samples from the best locations.

The Safaridb application itself is a multi-faceted application encompassing upload tools, soft-data(images, text, 3d-model viewing) search and display functionality, hard-data (numerical data extraction like graphs and statistics) search and display functionality. OMTT implements all aspects of the Safaridb application except for the 3d-viewer which is made by the project owners themselves, the University of Aberdeen and Uni Research AS in Bergen.

Videonor

Videonor is Norwegian cloud provider of video conferencing solutions operating globally with sales departments in Boston, Norway, Brussel and Sweden. OMTT is currently working on improving and automating internal processes and developing new products for them.

Hubbit

Hubbit is a brand new startup aiming to make digital signage a low cost product using modern platforms for content delivery and providing a very easy to use administration panel where you can manage multiple remote screens showing a wide variety of content like videos, pictures, presentation slides or audio. OMTT is developing the product suite of Hubbit in its entirety and is slated to release into market around february 2016.
